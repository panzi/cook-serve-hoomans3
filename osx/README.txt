Cook, Serve, Hoomans! 3
=======================

To apply this mod just right click -> open cook_serve_hoomans3.command. If that
gives you and error and can't find the Cook, Serve, Delicious! 3 installation double
click open_with_cook_serve_hoomans3.command and manually choose the game archive.

Usually the game archive is called "game.ios" and located somewhere in your
Cook, Serve, Delicious! installation directory.
